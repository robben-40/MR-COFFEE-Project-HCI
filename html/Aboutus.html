CEO & Founder

Susanto Liman

Susanto Liman, the heart and soul behind MR.COFFEE, was once a traveler with a deep love for coffee and human connection. After years of exploring remote villages and tasting rare brews, he dreamed of a place where coffee wasn’t just a drink—but a bridge between people. With a small savings and a big vision, he opened MR.COFFEE in the heart of the city, filling it with mismatched chairs, local art, and an unshakable belief that every cup should tell a story.

Our Mission

To craft more than coffee—to create moments of warmth, inspiration, and connection. Every bean, every brew, and every conversation at MR.COFFEE is designed to make strangers feel like friends and ordinary days feel extraordinary.

Our Vision

To build a global community of coffee lovers who find joy in shared stories, creativity, and the simple magic of a perfectly brewed cup. Beyond brick-and-mortar, MR.COFFEE aims to be a digital haven where this spirit thrives.

Company Story

What began as a tiny shop with creaky floors and doodle-covered walls soon became a sanctuary for dreamers, artists, and wanderers. The aroma of ethically sourced beans and the hum of laughter turned MR.COFFEE into a local legend. Now, Susanto’s dream expands—bringing the same warmth online, so no matter where you are, you can always pull up a chair (virtual or real) and feel at home.
