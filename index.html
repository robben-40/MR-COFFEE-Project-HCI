Susanto Liman, the heart and soul behind MR.COFFEE, was once a traveler with a deep love for coffee and human connection. He dreamed of a place where coffee wasn’t just a drink—but a bridge between people. With a small savings and a big vision, he opened MR.COFFEE in the heart of the city, filling it with mismatched chairs, local art, and an unshakable belief that every cup should tell a story.
